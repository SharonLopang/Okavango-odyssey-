Our services

At Ogavango odyssey, we offer a wide range of exciting, educational, and unforgettable tourism services designed to showcase the beauty, culture, and wildlife of Botswana. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, we've got something just for you.

  • Mokoro Canoe Safari: Glide silently through the waterways of the Okavango Delta in a traditional mokoro (dugout canoe) while enjoying close-up views of birds, hippos, and breathtaking landscapes. This is a peaceful and magical way to explore nature.
  • Helicopter Scenic Flights: Soar above the Okavango Delta and witness its beauty from the skies. These flights offer stunning aerial views of the wetlands, animal movements, and natural wonders that can't be seen from the ground.
  • Night Game Drives: Discover the wild after dark. Our night safaris give you the rare chance to see nocturnal animals like hyenas, bush babies, leopards, and owls in their natural habitat, using safe spotlight technology.
  • Luxury Lodge Stays: Stay in high-end lodges that offer comfort, elegance, and fantastic views of the wilderness. These lodges come with excellent dining, guided walks, and private decks for stargazing and relaxation.
  • Cultural Village Experiences: Meet the local people and learn about traditional Botswana life. From food tasting and crafts to dancing and storytelling, you'll get to experience authentic culture in a respectful and immersive way.
  • Walking Safaris: Walk safely through the bush with expert guides who teach you how to read animal tracks, identify plants, and understand wildlife behavior. It's a great way to connect deeply with nature.
  • Custom Private Tours: Want something more personal? We offer fully customized tours based on your interests, schedule, and budget. Whether you're a photographer, birder, or family group, we can create the perfect adventure for you.
  • Airport Transfers and Travel Support: From the moment you land, we've got you covered. We offer comfortable airport pick-up and drop-off services to make your travel stress-free.

    • We are proud to offer professional, eco-friendly, and culturally respectful services that leave a positive impact on both our guests and the environment. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities.

    Thank you for choosing Ogavango odyssey. We appreciate your trust and we are here to make every moment of your tour smooth, enjoyable, and truly unforgettable. Let's create memories together in the heart of the Okavango!
