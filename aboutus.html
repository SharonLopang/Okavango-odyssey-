About Us

Welcome to Okavango odyssey - your trusted local tour guide service dedicated to showcasing the natural beauty, culture, and adventure of Botswana's most stunning destinations. We specialize in personalized, educational, and unforgettable tours, with a special focus on the Okavango Delta and surrounding regions.

Who We Are

Okavango odyssey was founded by a team of passionate locals who grew up exploring the rich wildlife and landscapes of Botswana. With years of experience in the tourism industry, we bring you authentic knowledge and genuine love for the land. Our guides are trained, licensed, and committed to giving every visitor a safe and enriching experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people with the heart of Botswana through eco-friendly, culturally respectful, and exciting adventures. We aim to promote sustainable tourism that benefits both travelers and local communities.
